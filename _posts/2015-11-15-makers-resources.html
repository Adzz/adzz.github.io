This will be a collection of resources and utilities that I think might be helpful to Makers and really anyone coding, or trying to learn how to. Updated as and when.

Reading

A List Apart

Fantastic mailing list/collection of articles about (mostly) front end web design and ux. Some personal highlights include: Quantity Queries For CSS and Axiomatic CSS and Lobotomized Owls. Also a really good resource to find people to follow on…..

Twitter

Most people have twitter, and when you have an area or industry to be involved with it’s way better. Great resource for finding new articles, news, products…. and hilarious dog vines

What Is Code

Just read it. Best article of the year.

CSS Tricks

Really great website about all things CSS, very current and a large archive of almost anything you would want to know.

Breaking Smart

As they say: “a binge-worthy collection of essays”. Thought provoking and intelligent.

Learning

Treehouse

Full of videos and interactive courses for a pretty decent price. Good for beginners.

Codeacademy

Goes without saying, great opening gambit.

Utilities

F.Lux

Great for tired eyes, makes your screen the same brightness as your background light. try it out, really works, not great for colour work but really good for late night code-a-thons.

Pocket

Superb app for saving articles to read offline. Can sync form phone to desktop to tablet.
