I take it all back.

Don’t do it.

Follow the rules, do as you’re told. Don’t install zsh, don’t try and be a smart arse. It’s all too hard!

But seriously, it may have been several hours by now but after a few full uninstalls I HAVE A WORKING DEVELOPMENT ENVIRONMENT!

Well except for the Sublime command line tool, so instead I’ve decided to use Atom. For now.

I have definitely learnt a lot, but sometimes what you learn isn’t always applicable to the work you’ve been set. Sometimes you find working harder to be at the same level as others isn’t worth it. Of course you don’t know this until after, but still. From now on a good motto might be to keep it simple.

We’ll see.
